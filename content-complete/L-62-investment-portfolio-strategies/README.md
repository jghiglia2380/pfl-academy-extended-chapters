# L-62: Investment Portfolio Strategies - Chapter Summary

**Status:** ✅ COMPLETE
**L-Chapter:** 62
**Category:** Advanced Financial Literacy Topics
**Tier:** Tier 3 - Fully Agnostic (universal investment strategies)

## Overview
L-62 advances from L-47's investment types foundation to strategic portfolio construction. Students learn asset allocation, diversification strategies, rebalancing techniques, tax optimization, and comprehensive portfolio management.

**Usage:** Required by Louisiana; available for all states
**Prerequisite:** L-47 (Introduction to Investment Types)

## Structure

### Day 1: Portfolio Strategy Fundamentals (55 min)
- Asset allocation as primary performance driver
- Diversification across and within asset classes
- Rebalancing techniques and benefits
- Tax-efficient investing strategies
- Real examples: Emma (rebalancing), David (tax optimization), Sofia (lifecycle evolution)

### Day 2: Portfolio Construction Lab (55 min)
1. Build three lifecycle portfolios (young/mid-career/pre-retirement)
2. Test through historical scenarios (2008, 2020, 1970s)
3. Rebalancing simulation (5-year comparison)
4. Tax optimization exercise (asset location)
5. Personal investment policy statement creation

## Key Features

### Educational Approach
- Builds directly on L-47 investment types knowledge
- Evidence-based strategies (historical data throughout)
- Emphasis on systematic process over market timing
- Balance between optimization and simplicity

### Interactive Tools
1. **Advanced Portfolio Builder** - Multi-section comprehensive tool
2. **Historical Scenario Tester** - Test portfolios through actual market events
3. **Rebalancing Simulator** - 5-year comparison
4. **Tax Efficiency Optimizer** - Asset location calculator
5. **Investment Policy Statement Generator** - Personalized plan creation

### Core Concepts
- Asset allocation explains >90% of performance variance
- Lifecycle allocation evolution (aggressive → conservative with age)
- Rebalancing enforces "buy low, sell high" systematically
- Tax optimization can add 0.5-1.5% annually (compounds to massive differences)
- Written investment plans dramatically improve outcomes

## Content Highlights
- Age-based allocation rules (110 minus age in stocks)
- Diversification across sizes, sectors, geographies
- Annual vs. threshold-based vs. contribution-based rebalancing
- Asset location optimization (bonds in IRA, stocks in taxable)
- Three-fund portfolio simplicity (U.S. stocks, international, bonds)
