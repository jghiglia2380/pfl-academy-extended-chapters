### Alternative Coverage

**REITs: Legitimate, Accessible**
- Real estate exposure without property management
- Appropriate for many portfolios (5-15% allocation)
- Index funds provide broad diversification

**Commodities/Gold: Limited Role**
- Potential inflation hedge (5-10% max)
- No income generation
- Extreme volatility, long underperformance periods

**Cryptocurrency: Extreme Caution**
- Highly speculative, not core investing
- If allocated at all: 1-5% maximum
- Must be money you can lose completely
- Historical: Multiple 80% crashes

**Derivatives/Leverage: Warning**
- Professional tools, inappropriate for most retail investors
- High risk of total loss
- Speculation, not wealth-building

## Core Concepts

**Key Insights:**
- Traditional 60/40 or 80/20 portfolios build substantial wealth
- Warren Buffett recommends S&P 500 index for most people
- Complexity creates opportunities for fees, mistakes, poor decisions
- Small alternative allocations (10-20% total) potentially add diversification
- Large alternative allocations usually reduce returns and increase risk

**Decision Framework:**
Before any alternative investment, check:
- [ ] Do I fully understand it?
- [ ] Can I afford complete loss?
- [ ] Does it serve specific portfolio purpose?
- [ ] Have I compared to traditional alternatives?
- [ ] Am I investing from strategy or FOMO?

Only invest if ALL boxes checked.
