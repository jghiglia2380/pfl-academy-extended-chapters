# L-63: Advanced Investment Concepts - Assets Specification

## Day 1 Assets

### 1. Alternative Investment Comparison Matrix
**Purpose:** Side-by-side comparison of alternatives vs. traditional investments

**Format:** Interactive table

**Comparison Factors:**
- Historical returns (10-year, 30-year)
- Volatility (standard deviation)
- Correlation with stocks/bonds
- Liquidity (how quickly sold)
- Fees (typical expense ratios)
- Tax treatment
- Complexity level
- Appropriate allocation range

**Investments Compared:**
- Stocks (S&P 500)
- Bonds (Aggregate Bond Index)
- REITs
- Gold
- Diversified Commodities
- Bitcoin
- Total alternative funds

## Day 2 Assets (Learning Lab)

### 1. Alternative Investment Analyzer (Primary Skill Builder)

**Section A: Real Estate vs. REIT Comparator**
- Input property details (price, down payment, rent, expenses)
- Calculate cash-on-cash return, total return, time commitment
- Compare to REIT ETF investment
- Show 10-year projected outcomes
- Provide pros/cons analysis

**Section B: Cryptocurrency Portfolio Simulator**
- Test different crypto allocations (0%, 2%, 5%, 10%)
- Model historical volatility (gains and crashes)
- Show impact on overall portfolio
- Calculate "can you afford to lose this?" assessment
- Provide risk warnings and education

**Section C: Alternative Asset Diversification Analyzer**
- Build portfolios with various alternative combinations
- Calculate expected returns and volatility
- Show correlation benefits
- Assess whether complexity is justified by improvement
- 30-year projection comparison

**Section D: Alternative Investment Decision Framework**
- Guided evaluation tool for any alternative opportunity
- Checklist: Understanding, Appropriateness, Costs, Risks, Alternatives
- Pass/fail assessment
- Red flag identifier
- Recommendation engine

### 2. Historical Alternative Performance Database

**Purpose:** Research how alternatives actually performed historically

**Data Included:**
- REITs (1972-present)
- Gold (1970-present)
- Commodities (1990-present)
- Bitcoin (2010-present)

**Interactive Features:**
- Select time period
- Compare to stocks/bonds
- See best/worst years
- Understand volatility visually

**Key Teaching Data:**
- Gold 1980-2000: Declined from $850 to $250 (20-year loss)
- Bitcoin: Multiple 80% crashes
- REITs 2008: Fell 39% (worse than stocks)
- REITs 2009-2019: Outperformed stocks

### 3. REIT Analysis Tool

**Purpose:** Evaluate REIT sectors and individual REITs

**Inputs:** REIT sector or individual REIT ticker

**Outputs:**
- Property type (residential, commercial, industrial, etc.)
- Dividend yield
- Historical returns
- Interest rate sensitivity
- Economic cycle sensitivity
- Comparison to REIT index

**Educational Value:** Show diversification within real estate

---

### 4. Leverage Calculator (Educational, not Promotional)

**Purpose:** Demonstrate risks of leverage

**Scenarios:**
- Margin loan on stocks
- Leveraged ETF
- Real estate mortgage leverage
- Cash investment (no leverage)

**Show for each:**
- Gains when market rises 10%, 20%, 30%
- Losses when market falls -10%, -20%, -30%
- Margin call scenarios
- Total loss scenarios

**Key Teaching:** Leverage magnifies BOTH gains and losses

---

### 5. Cryptocurrency Education Module

**Purpose:** Factual education about cryptocurrency (not promotion)

**Content:**
- How blockchain works (simplified)
- Bitcoin vs. Ethereum vs. altcoins
- Historical price volatility
- Regulatory uncertainty
- Security risks (hacking, lost passwords)
- Tax implications
- Environmental concerns
- Legitimate use cases vs. speculation

**Interactive Quiz:**
- True/False: Cryptocurrency is backed by government (False)
- True/False: You can lose 100% of crypto investment (True)
- What % of portfolio do most advisors recommend IF investing? (1-5% max)

### 3. Alternative Investment Decision Checklist (1 page)
**Questions to ask before any alternative:**
- [ ] Do I fully understand how this works?
- [ ] What drives returns and losses?
- [ ] Can I afford complete loss?
- [ ] What are the fees?
- [ ] How liquid is this?
- [ ] Have I compared to traditional alternatives?
- [ ] Am I investing from strategy or FOMO?
- [ ] Does this fit my overall plan?

### 4. Cryptocurrency Risk Assessment (1 page)
**Content:**
- "Can you afford to lose this?" calculator

- Appropriate allocation guidelines (0-5% max)
- Warning signs of FOMO-driven investing

## Technical Requirements

**Data Requirements:**
- Historical prices: stocks, bonds, REITs, gold, Bitcoin (1970-present where available)
- REIT database (sectors, individual REITs, dividend yields)
- Cryptocurrency market data (prices, volatility, correlations)
- Alternative investment correlations matrix

**Calculations:**
- Real estate cash-on-cash return
- Leveraged return amplification
- Portfolio volatility with alternatives
- Cryptocurrency extreme scenario modeling
- Tax implications of various alternatives

**User Experience:**
- Warning messages for high-risk allocations
- Educational pop-ups explaining risks
- Comparison toggles (with/without alternatives)
- Mobile-responsive design
- Print-friendly formats

**Educational Focus:**
- All tools should educate, not promote
- Risk warnings prominent
- Comparison to traditional alternatives always shown
- "You don't need this" messaging integrated

---

## Development Priority
1. Alternative Investment Analyzer (comprehensive primary tool)
2. Historical Alternative Performance Database (show reality, not hype)
3. Cryptocurrency Education Module (critical risk education)
4. Printable Decision Checklist
5. Investment Sequence Completion resources

---

**Design Philosophy:** Tools should empower informed decision-making, not encourage speculation. Every interactive element should help students understand: "Do I need this alternative, or is my traditional portfolio sufficient?" For most, the answer is: traditional portfolio is excellent.
