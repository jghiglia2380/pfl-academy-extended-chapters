## Content Highlights:

### Real-World Examples:

**Maria's Rental Property Journey:**
- 28-year-old saves $45K, buys $220K duplex
- House-hacks (lives in one unit, rents other)
- 5 years later: $115K equity + $600/month cash flow
- Demonstrates real estate wealth-building with realistic numbers

**Jordan's Balanced Approach:**
- 25-year-old with solid foundation ($10K emergency fund, 15% retirement)
- Strategic alternatives: 5% REIT, 3% gold, 2% crypto
- Rules-based discipline prevents speculation
- 5-year outcome: $42K portfolio with controlled risk

**Alex's Cryptocurrency Lesson:**
- 22-year-old invests entire $8K savings in crypto at peak
- Loses $5,600 + opportunity cost of $115K (if invested in index funds)
- Powerful cautionary tale about speculation vs. investing
- Teaches: foundation first, understand investments, only risk what you can lose

### Pedagogical Strengths:

1. **Experiential Learning:** Crypto simulation creates visceral emotional responses that stick
2. **Comparative Analysis:** Direct property vs. REIT calculator shows tradeoffs concretely
3. **Data-Driven Discovery:** Correlation explorer lets students find patterns themselves
4. **Personal Application:** Decision framework creates individual commitments
5. **Balanced Perspective:** Neither promotes nor dismisses alternatives—informed choice
